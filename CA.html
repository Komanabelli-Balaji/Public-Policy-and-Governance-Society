About Us

The Campus Ambassador Program is launched with the aim of developing an Governance culture in various colleges across the country.

It is a Pan-India program for onboarding student ambassadors to not only publicize the events conducted by PPGS IIT KHARAGPUR but will also help them learn about Governance as a whole.


Aim

We aim to develop future leaders with an ... mindset, well versed in various skills, including leadership, marketing, public speaking, business development, etc. With free seats in various events and lecture series given by various founders and CEOs, CAP aims at an all-around development as an individual.

FAQ

What is the time period of the programme ?

It will take place from March 2024 to June 2024 (4 months)

Will all the participants receive a certificate?

The number of certificates is not subject to any limitations. As long as the ambassador completes the program and meets the requirements outlined in the credit system, a certificate will be given to them. We will choose them based on their passion and interest in the program.

What skills can be gained through this program?

We strongly believe on successful completion of your tenure; you will be well versed in communication & management skills, marketing skills, leadership skills, and many more.

What is the selection procedure?

There will be an application shortlisting followed by an interview wherein you will be judged based on your interest and enthusiasm for the program.

What incentives will I get from the program?

Top-performing Campus Executive can enjoy the benefits of the incentives which are mentioned

What does learning modules consist of and how would it help me?

In addition to the outlined topics featured in the brochure, our program encompasses a diverse array of discussions led by seasoned professionals. These sessions are designed to facilitate professional development, offering valuable insights and fostering familiarity with forthcoming tasks and responsibilities.

What would be the judging metrics for ranking of the Campus Executive?

Campus Executive will be judged on the basis of a credit system which will be intimated in detail after the recruitment process.
