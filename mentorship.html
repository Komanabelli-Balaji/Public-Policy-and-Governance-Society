What is Mentorship
Program ?


Mentorship Program is a guiding light for serious UPSC aspirants. We connect students with recent young officers who are proud alumni of IIT Kharagpur, offering invaluable mentorship, periodic interactive sessions, and dedicated support throughout their preparation journey.


Vision


The primary aim of the Mentorship Program is to empower UPSC aspirants by providing them with personalized guidance, insights, and unwavering support, enhancing the quality of their exam preparation, instilling confidence and motivation, and ultimately enabling them to achieve their goal of becoming civil servants.

Selection Procedure


  • Eligibility Criteria:

    The selection procedure will begin by identifying eligible candidates who are planning to appear in the UPSC examination in the following year. Only these individuals will be considered for participation in the program.

  • Mentorship Test:

    Eligible candidates will be required to take a mentorship test that assesses their knowledge and problem-solving abilities, focusing specifically on the static portions of the UPSC Prelims and Mains syllabus.

  • Performance Evaluation:

    Selection for the program will be determined based on the candidates' performance in the mentorship test. Those who perform well in the test will be considered for mentorship.

  • Mentor Assignment:

    Candidates who score satisfactorily in the mentorship test will be assigned a mentor, who will provide guidance, support, and direction throughout the program.

Rules

  • Participants must demonstrate a commitment to active engagement with their mentors, attending program activities, and following the prescribed study schedule.
  • All interactions within the program, including mentorship sessions, must be treated with strict confidentiality to protect the privacy of mentors and participants.
  • Participants must adhere to the guidelines, rules, and schedules provided by the program organizers.
  • Public Policy & Governance has the final authority to remove anyone from the program on the basis of performance and rule adherence.
