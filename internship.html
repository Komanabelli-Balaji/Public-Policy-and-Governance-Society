Why Public Policy Intern?

Practical Exposure

Interning in the field of public Policy provides a unique opportunity to bridge the gap between academia and real-world Policy-making. It allows students to apply their theoretical knowledge to practical scenarios, thereby enhancing their understanding of Policy formulation and implementation.

Impactful Contribution

Public Policy internships often involve working on projects that have a direct impact on society. As an intern, your work could potentially influence Policy decisions, making your contribution significant and rewarding.

Networking Opportunities

Interning in this field provides an excellent platform to connect with Policy-makers, researchers, and professionals in the sector. These connections can be invaluable for future career opportunities and collaborations.

Broaden horizons

As you get to interact with other interns and colleagues in that Public Policy, you come to know about other domains you could work in, and eventually you might just end up pursuing a stream that you find more fascinating and fulfilling.

About Public Policy Internship

Internship Programme is designed to empower students by bridging the gap between academia and real-world public Policy internships & projects. We offer comprehensive guidance on various public Policy internships, including eligibility criteria and CV enhancement strategies. Our guidance extends to helping students understand the significance of contributing to the field, ensuring that their journey into public Policy is both fulfilling and impactful.
