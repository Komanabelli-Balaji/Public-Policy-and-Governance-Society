ABOUT

Policy Research initiative is a testament to our commitment to intellectual exploration. Through rigorous research, we craft insightful blogs on governance issues, connecting the dots across various domains. Uniform Civil Code


Explore the essence of a Uniform Civil Code, envisioning a legal landscape where marriage, divorce, and inheritance transcend religious boundaries. Unveil the historical roots, from the Lex Loci Report in 1840 to the post-colonial era, where leaders like Nehru and Ambedkar advocated for a UCC in Article 44. Witness pivotal reforms, such as the Hindu Code Bill, and amendments in 2005 to the Hindu Succession Act, marking strides towards legal equality in a diverse society.

Public Procuremnt

Revolutionizing public procurement through advanced technologies is the key to unlocking the potential of good governance. From streamlined processes to enhanced transparency, technology serves as a catalyst for efficiency and accountability in public procurement. Discover the transformative impact technology holds in optimizing public procurement and paving the way for a more accountable and effective government.

Emergency


Remember when India went full "Big Brother"? We're spilling the tea on the Emergency's wildest secrets, from Indira's power play to silenced streets. Dive into secret resistance, media gags, and the chilling atmosphere. But hey, we'll also reveal the unexpected side effects, like vanishing strikes and family planning boom! Buckle up for a historical rollercoaster, separating fact from fiction.

Diplomatic Discourse
Series: A Synopsis

Unfolding the Series

The Diplomatic Discourse Series is a pioneering initiative aimed at bridging a world united in thought and action. It seeks to engage with esteemed bureaucrats from various embassies, focusing on non-controversial subjects like tourism and sustainable development, thereby promoting global harmony and understanding.

Goals and Aspirations

Our Diplomatic Discourse Series is a journey towards building stronger international relationships. We aspire to create a platform for open dialogue and knowledge sharing, enhancing mutual understanding and cooperation. Through this series, we strive to inspire change, contribute to a more informed world, and make a difference in the global community.


The Need for Diplomatic Discourse

In an increasingly interconnected world, diplomatic discourse is essential. It allows us to understand different perspectives, build mutual respect, and work together towards common goals. This series could be the stepping stone towards creating a way for collaborative solutions to global challenges and creating a more inclusive and understanding global society.

Competitions

Decoding the Indian Budget

Decoding the Indian budget is the competition to engage members in a comprehensive analysis of the Indian budget, encouraging them to identify and scrutinize the existing fiscal framework of 2023-24.

Argument Arena Debate

Argument Arena debate competition is designed to cultivate critical thinking and hone participants' skills in Policy and law making. Engaging in structured arguments allows participants to delve into complex issues, analyze different perspectives, and articulate well-reasoned positions.


Unveiling Indian State: A Holistic Exploration

Conduct an in-depth analysis of a chosen Indian state, encompassing aspects such as basic information, living standards, culture, identity, economic indicators, trends, political background, leadership analysis, governance, lawmaking, trade connections, and challenges and opportunities.
