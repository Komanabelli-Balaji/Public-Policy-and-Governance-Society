India-Thailand Ties Interview

  • An interview with consul of Thailand, MR.Pichaya

It is with immense pleasure and honor that we introduce our Chief Guest, Mr. Pichaya. He is currently serving as Consul of Thailand. This is the ranking of diplomatic officers posted here in India. Following are his thoughts and answers on various common doubts have a glimpse of what work Consul do day by day so that you can have a better understanding on the work of diplomacy.

War is the failure of diplomacy. I think this quote best sums up what diplomacy, public policy and international relations is all about. Hello, everyone. Interviewee:
