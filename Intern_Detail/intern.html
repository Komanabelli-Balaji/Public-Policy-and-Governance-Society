Internship Program

Internship Programme is designed to empower students by bridging the gap between academia and real-world public Policy internships & projects.

We offer comprehensive guidance on various public Policy internships, including eligibility criteria and CV enhancement strategies.

Our guidance extends to helping students understand the significance of contributing to the field, ensuring that their journey into public Policy is both fulfilling and impactful.
